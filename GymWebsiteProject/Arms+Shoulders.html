Arms and Shoulders

Incline Dumbbell Curl(Bicep)

This Exercise allows for a greater range of motion compared to other exercises. Remember to not sway your elbow.

Tricep V-bar Pushdown (Tricep)

This is by far my favorite tricep exercise. I like the factor that you have to use your core for stability, but it could be a limiting factor for those who have a weak core.

Dumbbell Lateral Raises (Delt)

When raising the dumbbell, imagine you are pouring tea on both hands. I like to pause at the top of this movement
